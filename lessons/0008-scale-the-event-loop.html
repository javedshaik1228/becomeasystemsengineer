Check only after answering aloud

TCP preserves an ordered byte stream, not message boundaries. Ownership must transfer once and remain visible. Decode and validate the length against a configured maximum before allocating its payload.

Compact mental model

Readiness is permission to try an operation—not proof that a whole request can finish.
